About Us

Welcome to Grind BBQ, where the smoke is thick, the sauce is slick, and the ribs are worth the trip!

Menu

Starters

  • Smoked Wings – Dry-rubbed and slow-smoked, served with ranch or blue cheese.
  • BBQ Nachos – Tortilla chips piled high with pulled pork, cheese sauce, jalapeños, and BBQ drizzle.
  • Fried Pickles – Crispy dill pickles with a side of house-made dipping sauce.

Signature BBQ Plates

  • Memphis Dry-Rub Ribs – Half rack or full rack, seasoned to perfection with our signature dry rub.
  • Wet Ribs – Smoked and slathered with our tangy house BBQ sauce.
  • Pulled Pork Plate – Tender, slow-smoked pork shoulder served with two sides.
  • Beef Brisket Plate – Sliced brisket smoked low and slow, served with two sides.
  • BBQ Chicken Plate – Half chicken smoked and basted with our secret BBQ glaze.

Build Your Own Plate

Choose 1 Meat, 2 Sides, and 1 Sauce:

  • Meats: Pulled Pork, Brisket, Smoked Sausage, BBQ Chicken, or Ribs (add $3 for ribs).
  • Sides: Coleslaw, Mac & Cheese, Baked Beans, Potato Salad, Collard Greens, or Cornbread.
  • Sauces: Memphis Tangy, Sweet Heat, Spicy Mustard, or Carolina Vinegar.

Sandwiches
